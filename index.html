About Me

I’m Viktoria Pyrih — a high fashion muse with Eastern European roots and a New York state of mind. Born in Ukraine, refined in global fashion capitals, I embody contrast: softness and power, classic beauty and cutting-edge attitude.

I don’t just pose — I transform. I’ve walked into Vogue pages, collaborated with visionary creatives, and brought energy to every campaign I join. Fashion is my language. Let me speak it on your runway, your set, your story.

My Story

From a small Ukrainian town to the heart of NYC, my journey is fueled by vision and fire. I am the muse brands remember, the silhouette that turns into moodboards.

I’ve moved cities with nothing but passion and entered rooms that weren’t made for me — then made them mine. Every project I join becomes a visual legacy. Every shot is a signature.
